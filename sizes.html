Spring Awakening hoodies

Sizes

The hoodies are available in extra small, small, medium, large, extra large, extra-extra large and extra-extra-extra large. Chest sizes for each can be found below.

Please note: The black and lilac hoodies come in slightly different measurements. Make sure you know which colour you want before choosing the best size.

XS S M L XL XXL XXXL
Black 34″ 37″ 40″ 43″ 46″ 50″ 53″
Lilac 34″ 36″ 40″ 44″ 48″ 52″ 56″

The measurements above are ‘to fit’ the given chest measurement. This means the hoodies will be larger than the size above to ensure it fits the chest size.
